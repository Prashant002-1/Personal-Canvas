# Mission

## What We're Building

A smart study tool for students — one that doesn't just generate a plan, but acts as a partner.

When a student sits down with their laptop and opens their syllabus, they stare at the screen and wonder where to start. We've all been there. This tool was built for that moment.

It connects directly to Canvas, reacts to new changes, updates the plan, and talks you through it. It looks at your syllabus, figures out the best course of action for the exam two weeks away and the assignment due tonight. It suggests resources for things you may want to revisit outside of class. It ensures you're successful and making the most of your time.

Everything is persistent. It remembers your decisions, behaviors, and patterns. It improves with you.

## Scope

This is a hackathon demo. The goal is a working, presentable prototype with a clean UI and predictable states — not a production application. Every decision should serve the demo.
